Educators

What is Game Haven?
Game Haven is the place for middle school students to sharpen their skills in mathematics and problem solving. The games on our site are designed to incentivise independent mathematics learning through engaging mini-games and the opportunity to win tickets to theme parks.
Who can play on this site?
Only students who have return a parent-signed permission slip to their teachers will have access to login and play games on the site.
Why should a school district enroll in Game Haven?
A school disrict should enroll their students in Game Haven if they require a means of delivering incentivised and monitored education to students over summer break and through the academic year.
Where can I view my school's progress?
You can view individualized student performance by logging into the educators portal.
