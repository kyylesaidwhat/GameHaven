Theme Park Affiliation

Students have the opportunity to earn enough in-game points to acquire tickets to a Game Haven affiliated theme park.

Rewarding hard-working students with tickets to amusement parks is a great way to encourage self-driven learning of mathematics and to invest in the future leaders of tomorrow.

If you are a theme park franchise holder, and would like to make your theme park a Game Haven affiliated theme park, click here.
