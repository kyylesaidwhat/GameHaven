Disney Orlando

The Walt Disney World Resort is an entertainment complex in Bay Lake and Lake Buena Vista, Florida, in the United States, near the cities Orlando and Kissimmee.

Home to the Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom, Blizzard Beach and Typhoon Lagoon, the Walt Disney World Resort has something for everyone in the family to enjoy.
